J.R. Murphy

Shareholder

J.R. Murphy founded the firm of Murphy & Grantland with John Grantland in 1997. He serves as the managing shareholder and concentrates his legal practice on insurance coverage, bad faith and complex personal injury cases.

Originally from Virginia, J.R. attended the University of South Carolina on a soccer scholarship and obtained both his undergraduate and juris doctor degrees from the University. He has extensive experience in every practice area of the firm including automobile/ transportation defense, product liability, construction defect and employment law.

J.R. has argued more than 250 jury trials and is one of South Carolina's top appellate attorneys. He has brought close to 100 cases before the state and federal appellate courts including the South Carolina Supreme Court and Court of Appeals.

Clients and fellow attorneys frequently call upon J.R. for his evaluation of unusual insurance coverage matters, including auto and general liability and first party property coverage policies. He also counsels clients in how to avoid or defend extra contractual liability claims.

J.R. is a member of the Federation of Defense and Corporate Counsel and is a frequent lecturer at legal and insurance industry seminars around the country.
